Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Lime
  • Egg
  • Nutmeg
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Margarine
  • Yeast
  • Tacos leaves
  • good size of ham
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. 5ltrs of clean water of
  2. 5kg of any flour of your choice. be it maize, cassava or potatoe
  3. 1kg of sugar
  4. 20 peices of lime
  5. 1 crate of eggs
  6. 2 grated nutmeg into powder form
  7. 20 miligram of salt
  8. 10kg of Margarine
  9. 1 spoonful of yeast
  10. Miss all the Ingredients together to fine content and allow the uniform mixture to be.
  11. miss the uniform content with the water and trun the mixture to a dough.
  12. allow the yeast to work over the content by allowing it to settle over 5-10 hrs period.
  13. Then cut the yeast inflated dough into your likeable sizes
  14. Then set your well cut dough into the furnace, oven or any enclosed heat source
  15. Then allow your baked dough to settle
  16. Recipe is ready to serve
