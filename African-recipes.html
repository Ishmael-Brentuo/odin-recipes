My best continental recipe gallary featuring all continental recipes. But this special recipe is an African one, so delicious, prepared by professional African chefs. its so delicious you have to try some. thanks!

Thank you so much for listening to all the good sides of our dishes and making it a deal by adventuring to taste some of our delicious dishes. Thank you so much.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Cooking oil
  • Beans
  • green edible leaves
  • Milk or sprinking cream
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil the water with the rice in the boilling sauce
  2. Boil your beans and cook to become tender
  3. put a sizable amount of the cooked rice and beans in another sauce pan and put on fire and cook
  4. Mix all prepared ingredients at your disposal with the the cooked mixed rice and beans.
  5. Allow the mix to cook for a while before and make of a uniform mix and then turn off your gas fire.
  6. Then remember to serve the meal hot. to get the best taste
